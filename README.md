# Mist of the Emerald Isle

A Roguelite adventure where you must travel through the mist to save St. Patrick's Day.

## How to Play

1.  **Choose Your Path:** Navigate through the mist by selecting event cards. Each card leads to different outcomes and future paths.
2.  **Dice Challenges:** Many events require rolling dice. You roll 4 dice and must select exactly 2 for their instant effects. The remaining 2 dice become **Auras**.
3.  **Auras:** Auras provide passive benefits or penalties that apply before every event you encounter. You have a maximum of 6 aura slots.
4.  **Corruption:** Be careful! Corruption increases as you take risks. If your corruption reaches 10, the mist consumes you and the run ends.
5.  **High Corruption:** If your corruption reaches 7 or higher, the paths ahead become "High Corruption" cards. These offer 50% increased rewards but also 50% increased risks.
6.  **Character Profile:** Click the Trophy icon in the header to view your collected traits and their effects.

## Win Conditions

*   **Reach the End:** Survive through all layers of the mist.
*   **Defeat the Boss:** The final challenge is a 3-round dice showdown against a powerful foe.
*   **Save St. Patrick's Day:** Successfully complete the boss battle to clear the mist and save the holiday!

## Tips

*   **Balance Risk:** High corruption is dangerous but rewarding. Use it to your advantage if you have strong traits.
*   **Manage Auras:** Clovers help reduce corruption. Keep an eye on your aura slots to maintain a healthy balance.
*   **Traits Matter:** Collect traits from events to strengthen your character for the final battle.
